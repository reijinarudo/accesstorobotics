Support the Initiative

The Finley Robotics Initiative relies on the generosity of individuals and organizations who believe that every student deserves access to cutting‑edge technology. Your support helps us purchase Reachy Mini robots, provide mentorship programs and expand our reach to more schools and communities.

Ways to Give

  • Individual Donations: Every contribution—large or small—directly supports our scholars. You can sponsor part of a robot, an entire kit or ongoing mentorship services.
  • Corporate Partnerships: We welcome partnerships with companies interested in STEM education, diversity in tech and community engagement. Corporate sponsors may provide funding, equipment, volunteer mentors or internship opportunities.
  • In‑Kind Gifts: Donations of laptops, software licenses or lab space help our scholars experiment and build.

How to Donate

If you would like to contribute, please contact us using the form on our Contact page or send an email to drreginaldfinley‑phd@yahoo.com. We will provide instructions on how to make a tax‑deductible gift and share information about sponsorship levels.

Thank you for helping us inspire the next generation of inventors and engineers.

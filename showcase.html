Scholar Showcase

The record of the work, as it is produced.

Each scholar publishes project documentation here during the program year. The Showcase serves as a portfolio for the scholar and as the program's transparent record of outcomes.

Status

Awaiting the inaugural cohort.

The first scholar projects will be published here beginning Spring 2027, after the 2026–2027 cohort receives their hardware and starts work. Until then, this page is intentionally blank.

What will appear here

The format of a published project.

Each project entry will include: the scholar's name (or initials, by family preference), a written description of the problem they chose, the approach they took, the code or design files they produced, what worked, what did not, and where they ended the program year. Photographs and short video clips will be included when available.

We will not retouch project outcomes. A scholar who attempted a hard problem and did not finish it will be documented honestly. That documentation is more useful to the scholar's future, and to subsequent cohorts, than a sanitized version would be.

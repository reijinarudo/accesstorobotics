Expanding Access to Robotics

The Finley Robotics Initiative provides underserved students with the tools, mentorship and inspiration needed to explore robotics and artificial intelligence.

Learn About Our Scholarship

Our Mission

The Finley Robotics Initiative believes that curiosity and talent are not limited by geography or finances. Each year we offer a select group of high school students the opportunity to own and learn from a Reachy Mini robot—an expressive, open‑source companion designed for AI experimentation. By bridging the access gap, we hope to cultivate future engineers, scientists and creative thinkers.

What We Offer

Reachy Mini

Our scholars receive a brand‑new Reachy Mini robot to keep. Reachy Mini offers nine degrees of freedom, high‑resolution cameras, microphones and a Raspberry Pi compute module—making it an ideal platform for hands‑on robotics exploration.

Mentorship & Community

Recipients join a supportive learning community with regular virtual meetups, project challenges and access to guidance from experienced engineers and educators. We believe mentorship is just as important as the hardware itself.

Get Involved

Whether you are a student ready to apply, a school looking to partner or a supporter who wants to help us grow, we invite you to be part of the Finley Robotics Initiative.
